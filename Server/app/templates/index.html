ABOUT

Angyl is the premier warn-me system for home security and personal safety. Angyl continuously monitors your home and warns you in the event of fire or dire emergencies. Our personalized notification system utilizes local RSS Newsfeeds and electronic signals to add an extra layer of protection, keeping you and your family safe and secure. At Angyl, we plan on expanding our modular product line to include home, car, and bike protection services. Our job is to keep you safe--so you don't have to worry!
